2017 Honda Civic LX Grey

Style, Efficiency, and Reliability in One Package

Specifications

Engine

2.0L 4-Cylinder, 158 hp @ 6500 rpm, 138 lb-ft @ 4200 rpm

Bolts Pattern

The bolt pattern is 5x114.3 mm, also known as 5x4.5".

Fuel Economy

28 city / 40 highway / 32 combined (6-speed manual)

31 city / 40 highway / 34 combined (CVT)

Dimensions

Length: 182.3 in, Height: 55.7 in, Weight: ~2749 lbs

Transmission

6-speed manual or CVT

Cargo Space

15.1 cu.ft (trunk)

Safety

6 airbags, Multi-angle rearview camera, Vehicle Stability Assist

Key Features

  • Technology: 5-inch LCD screen, Bluetooth HandsFreeLink, USB audio interface.
  • Comfort: Cloth seats, spacious cabin with 42.3 in front legroom, 37.4 in rear legroom.
  • Safety: Honda Sensing available (Collision Mitigation, Lane Departure Warning).
  • Performance: Nimble handling, Eco Assist System for efficient driving.
  • Design: Sleek grey exterior, aggressive front grille, modern interior layout.

Maintenance Tips

  • Oil Change: Every 7,500 miles or 12 months.
  • Tire Rotation: Every 5,000-7,500 miles for even wear.
  • Brake Inspection: Check pads every 15,000 miles.
  • Air Filter: Replace every 30,000 miles or as needed.
